## Key Messaging Points

### What We Are
- AI replaces human CATI interviewers
- Real-time phone interviews (AI calls and asks questions)
- Same methodology as traditional CATI
- Same respondent experience (gets a call, answers questions)

### What We Are NOT
- ❌ Async voice surveys (people record on their own time)
- ❌ Self-service survey platform
- ❌ Chatbot or automated phone tree

### Value Proposition
1. **Same methodology** - Research protocols unchanged
2. **40-50% cheaper** - No interviewer labor costs (€20-30/hour)
3. **Faster** - No interviewer scheduling/training
4. **Scalable** - 100s of simultaneous calls
5. **Bilingual** - Finnish and Swedish

### Objection Handling

**"Will people accept talking to AI?"**
> "They won't know it's AI unless you tell them. The conversation feels natural, and the data quality is the same. We can run a small pilot to prove it."

**"What about complex questions?"**
> "AI can handle follow-up questions and probing just like a human interviewer. For highly complex interviews with many branches, we can work with you to structure the questions."

**"Is it GDPR compliant?"**
> "Yes. Same data handling as traditional CATI. We can sign DPA (Data Processing Agreement) like any other survey vendor."

**"What if people ask 'are you a robot?'"**
> "AI can handle that transparently: 'Yes, I'm an AI conducting this survey on behalf of [Organization]. Your responses are just as valuable.' Most people continue the interview."
