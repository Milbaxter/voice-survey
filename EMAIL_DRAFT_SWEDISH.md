## Recommended: Version 2 (Problem-Focused)

**Why:**
- Starts with a question (engages curiosity)
- Shows you've talked to similar orgs (social proof)
- Clear pain points they'll recognize
- Direct ask (15-min call)
- Personal but professional tone

## Personalization Tips

Before sending, customize:

1. **Replace [Namn]** with their actual first name
2. **Replace [Organisation]** with org name
3. **Add specific detail** based on their work:
   - Government: "för att följa språklagen" (to follow language law)
   - University: "för forskningsprojekt" (for research projects)
   - Media: "för att förstå er publik bättre" (to understand your audience better)
   - Market research: "för era kunder som behöver nå svenska talare" (for your clients who need to reach Swedish speakers)
