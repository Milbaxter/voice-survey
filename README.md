# Voice Survey - AI Phone Interview Service

**AI-powered phone interviews for research organizations in Finland**

Replace expensive human CATI interviewers with AI. Same methodology, same data quality, 40-50% cheaper.

---

## What We Do

We provide **AI phone interview services** that replace traditional CATI (Computer-Assisted Telephone Interviewing):

- **AI makes outbound calls** to respondents
- **Real-time conversation** - respondents answer questions verbally (just like human CATI)
- **Automatic transcription** in Finnish and Swedish
- **Structured data output** - same format as traditional phone surveys

**Target market:** Government research agencies, market research firms, universities, public sector organizations in Finland.

---

## Why AI Phone Interviews?

### vs Traditional CATI
- ✅ **40-50% cheaper** (no interviewer labor costs: €20-30/hour)
- ✅ **Faster deployment** (no interviewer recruitment/training/scheduling)
- ✅ **Scalable** (100s of simultaneous calls)
- ✅ **Consistent quality** (no interviewer variance)

### Same As CATI
- ✅ Phone interview methodology (research protocols unchanged)
- ✅ Real-time questioning and probing
- ✅ Structured data collection
- ✅ Same respondent experience (gets a call, answers questions)
