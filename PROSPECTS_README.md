## How to Use This Database

### Step 1: Pick 10 High-Priority Prospects
Start with the confirmed emails list above.

### Step 2: Personalize Email Template
Use the templates in OUTREACH_STRATEGY.md. Customize:
- Organization name
- Specific pain point for their sector
- Why it matters to them

### Step 3: Send Batch
Send 10 emails at once. Stagger by a few minutes so they don't look like bulk mail.

### Step 4: Track Responses
Update the JSON file or create a spreadsheet with columns:
- Date sent
- Response received? (Y/N)
- Call booked? (Date)
- Interest level (Hot/Warm/Cold)
- Notes

### Step 5: Follow Up
- Day 5: Send Email 2 (follow-up) to non-responders
- Day 10: Send Email 3 (final touch) to non-responders
- Book calls with anyone who responds

## Research Tips (For Missing Emails)

### Finding Contact Emails

**Method 1: Website Contact Page**
- Go to organization website
- Look for "Contact", "Kontakt", "Yhteystiedot"
- Find general email or department-specific email

**Method 2: Google Search**
```
"organization name" contact email site:.fi
"organization name" svenska email
```

**Method 3: LinkedIn**
- Search for organization
- Find employees (Communications Manager, Director, etc.)
- Get their LinkedIn profile
- Email format usually: firstname.lastname@domain.fi

**Method 4: Email Pattern Guessing**
Most Finnish organizations use these formats:
- firstname.lastname@domain.fi
- info@domain.fi
- kanslia@domain.fi (office/administration)
- kirjaamo@domain.fi (registrar)
- svenska@domain.fi (Swedish services)

**Method 5: Call the Switchboard**
- Use phone number from website
- Ask: "Vem kan jag kontakta angående marknadsundersökningar på svenska?" (Who can I contact regarding market research in Swedish?)

## Success Metrics

**Good response rate:** 15-30% (4-9 responses from 30 emails)  
**Great response rate:** 30%+ (9+ responses)  
**Target:** 5-10 calls booked, 1-2 verbal commitments

If response rate <10% after Week 1:
- Revise email subject line
- Shorten email body
- Try different value proposition
- Call instead of email
